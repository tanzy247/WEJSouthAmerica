Day 2

While in La Paz, we biked North Yungas Road (also known as death road).

It’s called death road because nearly 300 drivers were killed here every year until 1994. It was the main road that connected La Paz to a town called Coroico. However, they have now built a new one which is safer.


The route is a 64 km-long road with 3500 metres of descent. Every kids' dream. The longest downhill bike ride ever!

Uyuni

A day trip in Uyuni. First stop - abandoned trains. There were trains from GB, Germany and Japan, which apparently came here to get repaired but never did due to a war in Bolivia. Afterwards we visited the salt flats which was absolutely unreal!
